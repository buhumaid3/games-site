Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MathDen ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit mathden.com.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information automatically when you visit:

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages you visit and how long you stay
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region, not exact)
  • Referral source (how you found us)

We do not require signup and do not collect names, email addresses, or payment information from players.

Cookies and Tracking

MathDen uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember which games you recently played
  • Measure how people use the site (via PostHog analytics)
  • Display relevant ads through Google AdSense

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but parts of the site may not work correctly.

Advertising

We display ads through Google AdSense and partner ad networks. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your previous visits to this site or other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

Our games are provided by third-party game networks (including GameDistribution and similar partners). These services have their own privacy policies governing the data they collect while you play.

Children's Privacy

MathDen does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights

Depending on your location (EU, UK, California, etc.), you may have the right to access, delete, or restrict processing of your data. To exercise these rights, email hello@mathden.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.
